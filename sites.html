A List of Websites I Manage or Built

Some of these sites may not be supported by myself anymore. If a design changes that was not touched by me i will remove it from my list when it comes to my attention. Status: last checked March 2016

  • discovercounseling.com Status: Active Development

    A recommendation brought Discover Counseling to me for a redesign and reimplementation of WordPress. Aaron wanted to be able to continue managing content and with his familiarity of the basics of WP, we set out to get his design and content modernized and professional.

  • dahui.bar Status: Active Development

    Da' Hui Bar & Grill did not have a website when i approached Mike, the owner. We came up with a plan to build a site to advertise his catering service as well as simply getting the word out about the bar. We are discussing building out an online ordering system that is tied to his POS.

  • homesteadstove.com Status: Active Development

    Truth be told, my aunt owns Homestead Stove Company and has challenged me to learn much about web development. When i came on, they were using Netsuite, which at the time looked horrible and operated poorly. Initially building the site in C5, more recently we've switched to a WordPress installation to build out a web store which will always be under construction.

  • lifeencounter.com Status: Active Development

    Life Encounter Counseling needed a way to blog and wanted to update their web brand to accommodate mobile devices. After selecting a premium WordPress theme, i worked to migrate content from a static PHP site into what exists today. I've consulted Nathan on the importance of creating useful information for his potential clients via his blog in order to help boost his Google Search rank.

  • parkviewantiquitys.com Status: Static

    This is an eCommerce store i set up for a friend who wanted to be able to sell his collections of antiques. He was very specific in how he wanted to handle payments insofar that before a customer was able to place an order, he wanted them to email him to coordinate and discuss shipping.

  • cheezyflicks.com Status: Design Only

    Cheezy Flicks is a small reseller of old cheesy movies that people have nearly forgotten. They primarily sell DVDs that they author themeselves. I built this with the open-source OpenCart CMS and customized a theme template to match there "brand". They do not pay to have me support it any longer after a period of non-payment for my services. I wish them the best of luck.

  • nwipa.beer Status: Active Development

    N.W.I.P.A. is a beer tap bar located on Foster road. I noticed they were using WordPress.com so i thought i could bring them many more options for managing their website while improving their brand representation on the web. I'm currently working to re-train them on using the site as they have some difficulty keeping the tap list updated internally.

  • grindhousecomics.com Status: Design Only

    Grindhouse Comics is a side project for my old friends at Cheezy Flicks. I designed and developed their website using the UltimatumTheme.com, a framework for building simple and complex WordPress websites. Originally the site was going to have quite a bit more going on but they decided to keep it simple with a single page.


Check out some of my website strategy and design service at VisionMule.com
